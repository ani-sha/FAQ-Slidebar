FAQ SLIDER

  • Why has E-Shop re-launched its Desktop Site?
  • We have decided to bring back the desktop site to deliver the fast purchase experience. The biggest reason to re-launch the site is our Consumer feedback and our belief in empowering our customers with a convenient and easy way of shopping.
  • What is Try and Buy Service?
  • This service entitles you to try-out your purchases at the time of delivery, pay only for what you like and return the rest on-the-spot.
  • How do I cancel the order, I have placed?
  • You can cancel your order before it has been packed at our warehouse. If your order has already been shipped, you can refuse it at the time of delivery and refund will be processed into the source account, if order amount was paid online.
  • What is COD limit?
  • E-Shop reserves the right to determine the COD limit for every customer and/or orders. The COD limit includes any undelivered, unpaid orders and any new orders being placed.
  • How do I apply a coupon on my order?
  • You can apply a coupon on cart page before order placement. The complete list of your unused and valid coupons will be available under “My Coupons” tab of App/Website/M-site.
